Organization

Boost.RPC is designed to separate serialization, protocol, transport, and interface. So it should support JSON RPC over HTTP, TCP, SSL, UDP. It should also support Google Protocol Buffers over TCP, UDP, UDT, etc.

The order of abstraction is:

  • boost::reflect::any_ptr
    • json::client boost::rpc::json::connection json::connection #boost::rpc::json::tcp::connection json::tcp::connection
